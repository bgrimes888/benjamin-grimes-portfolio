About Me

I enjoy the creative and technical aspects of web development because it allows me to solve problems while also designing something visually engaging.

My goal is to develop strong front-end development skills and learn how to create websites that are both functional and user-friendly. I am especially interested in how websites are structured and styled to create clean layouts and intuitive navigation.

My Aspirations

My main aspiration is to become a skilled web developer who can build responsive, accessible, and visually appealing websites. As I continue learning, I plan to expand my knowledge beyond basic HTML and CSS and begin working with JavaScript and more advanced web technologies.
